About Feedbacker

The aim of feedbacker is to provide a tool which helps facilitate an open and transparent culture. Feedbacker itself is something of a combination of survey monkey and a workflow system.
Feedbacker supports the following features:

  • Configurable feedback/review cycles
  • Transparent sharing of feedback with recipients
  • Submission of ad-hoc feedback for a recipient
  • Email notifications
  • Consolidated views and reports of feedback for users
